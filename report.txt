   - MarketInstance model: 5min (4 instances), 10min (2 instances), 20min (1) per cycle
   - One global master clock (20-minute cycle)
   - Bet routing to correct market instance via durationMinutes, marketInstanceId
   - MarketInstanceSettlement, MarketSnapshot for idempotency
   - Premium users can select 5/10/20 min rounds
   - WebSocket: masterClockTick, aggregatedStatsUpdated, selectDuration
